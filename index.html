Coding is very intimidating for a lot of people but learning the basics and taking it step by step can make it easier for you to understand. An analogy to how a website works can be compared to a restaurant. A restaurant has a menu that people see (client side), a kitchen that serves the things on the menu (server), waiters who deliver to the customer (routing).
